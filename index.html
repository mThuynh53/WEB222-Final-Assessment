About Me

My name is Minh Tri Huynh. I was a Civil Engineering Technologist.

in May 2019, I started working for geotechnical and engineering material consulting company called Thurber Engineering Ltd. as a Quality Control/Assurance Inspector. My job is to checked if the construction materials (soils, concrete, asphalts, rebars) were good enough for a structure (this can be a road, a highway, a building, a drainage system, etc.)

When you realize your good day of the week is Friday and the bad day is Sunday, maybe something need to change. In March 2022, after almost 3 years working as a QA inspector, I decided I want to pursue another career. I want to get into Computer Programming. CS50 is the introduction to get into Computer Science and from it, I have learned a lot so far and felt the joy every time I solved a hard problems. Hopefully, I can learn more and more so that I can be skillful to get a job in this field.
